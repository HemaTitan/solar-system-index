Index

  • The solar system is the sun and everything that orbits around it.

  • The planets are Mercury, Venus, Earth, Mars, Jupiter, Saturn, Uranus and Neptune.

  • It includes the planets and their moons as well as numerous asteroids and comets.

  • Our solar system formed about 4.5 billion years ago from a dense cloud of interstellar gas and dust.

  • There are more than 150 known moons in our solar system and several more awaiting confirmation of discovery..
